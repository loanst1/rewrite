About ReWrite Lite

Important: ReWrite is a wellness support tool, not a medical device. It is designed to supplement — not replace — occupational therapy. Always follow the guidance of your therapist or care team.

What is ReWrite?

ReWrite Lite is the free version of ReWrite. It helps you rebuild handwriting skills through guided tracing exercises with letters and words. Designed for people recovering from stroke, brain injury, or other conditions that affect hand control and writing ability.

This is the free version of ReWrite with letter and word exercises. The full version includes numbers, sentences, and all difficulty levels. Available on the App Store, Google Play, and Amazon Appstore.

How it works

  • Choose an exercise type: individual letters or words (numbers and sentences in the full version)
  • A faint guide character appears on the writing area
  • Trace over the guide with your finger (touchscreen) or mouse
  • The app scores your accuracy based on how closely you traced the guide
  • Your progress is saved automatically on this device

Understanding benchmarks

Scores are compared against typical performance bands. These are guides only — visual, motor, cognitive and fatigue factors all affect scores. What matters most is your personal improvement over time.

Strong — close match to the guide
Developing — improving, keep practising
Early stage — every attempt builds new pathways

Tips for best results

  • Practice 10–15 minutes daily — short frequent sessions beat long infrequent ones
  • Start with individual letters before progressing to words and sentences
  • Focus on accuracy, not speed — you are retraining motor pathways
  • Use the Export button on the Progress tab to share results with your therapist
  • Sit comfortably with your device on a stable surface at a good angle

Privacy

All data is stored locally on your device only. Nothing is sent to any server. No account is needed. Your progress stays private.

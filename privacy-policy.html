ReWrite

Privacy Policy

Handwriting Rehabilitation App

Last updated: 16 April 2026

Summary: ReWrite stores everything on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no servers, and no tracking of any kind.

What ReWrite Is

ReWrite is a handwriting practice tool designed to support people recovering from stroke, brain injury, or other conditions that affect writing ability. It provides guided tracing exercises with progress tracking and colour-coded score bands.

Important: ReWrite is a wellness support tool, not a medical device. It is designed to supplement — not replace — occupational therapy. Always follow the guidance of your therapist or care team.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected (name, email, age, location, etc.)
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No crash reports sent to any server
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No cookies or tracking pixels
  • No health data is transmitted anywhere
  • No account creation or sign-in is required

Data Storage

All data generated by ReWrite — including exercise scores, session history, and settings — is stored locally on your device only using IndexedDB (a browser-based storage system). This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not synced to any cloud service
  • Is not accessible to us or anyone else
  • Can be deleted at any time using the "Reset All Progress" button in Settings

If IndexedDB is unavailable (for example, in private browsing mode), the app falls back to temporary in-memory storage. In this case, data is lost when you close the app, and a warning banner is shown.

Network Requests

ReWrite makes no network requests after the initial download. It works entirely offline. There is no backend server, no API calls, and no external resources loaded at runtime.

Third-Party Services

ReWrite uses no third-party services — no analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.), no advertising networks, no social media integrations, and no crash reporting tools.

Children's Privacy

ReWrite does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected or transmitted, the app complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act), GDPR-K, and equivalent regulations.

Health Data

While ReWrite generates handwriting exercise scores and progress data, this data is:

  • Stored exclusively on the user's device
  • Never transmitted to any server
  • Not classified as Protected Health Information (PHI) under HIPAA since it is never shared with, or accessible to, any covered entity
  • The "Export for Therapist" feature uses your device's built-in print function — no data is sent to ReWrite or any external service

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time:

  • In-app: Go to Settings → tap "Reset All Progress"
  • Via browser: Clear site data for the ReWrite domain in your browser settings
  • Via device: Uninstalling the app removes all associated data

Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations, you have rights regarding your personal data. Since ReWrite collects no personal data whatsoever, these rights are satisfied by default. There is nothing to access, correct, port, or delete on our end.

Age Rating

ReWrite is rated 4+ (Apple App Store) and Everyone (Google Play). The app contains no objectionable content, in-app purchases, advertisements, or social features.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date. Since ReWrite collects no data, significant changes are unlikely.
